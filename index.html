Here's what I have achieved so far.

Multimedia University of Kenya, BSc. in Mathematics and Computer Science.
I am currently a student here, class of 2019. I am an active member of the Computing and Information Technlogy (CIT) club. For the time I have been here, I have been able to attend several bootcamps and events that have been great eye openers. Beside presenting me with unprecedented scale and diversity of opportunity, MMU has also humbled me to explore my place in the world and adjusted my world view. You can find out more about MMU at their website..
Emobilis Institute of Mobile Technology, Mobile Software Development.
This is the best thing that ever happened to my life, I learned how to code. Although I am still learning more and more, this place presented me with the opportunity to discover my passion. I graduated with a certificate in Mobile Software Development (specializing in Android and Web) after going through their six month crash course. Besides, I learned how to effictively interact with people and express my ideas in an acceptable way. You can be like me if you wish, just start here.
Molo Academy Boys School, High School.
This was such a place! In four years of interacting with the phenomenal teachers in the various disciplines, I was PUSHED to engage my brains in creative ways. My experience in leadership as Class Secretary and Dorm Captain at different times showed me the power of empathetic leadership. Molo A. gifted me with a love for learning, sorrounded me with a wealth of inspiring peers and mentors and above all, strengthened my tolerance to adversity. Checkout Molo Academy's Website..

My skills at a glance.

Web Development and Design.
This is my way of expressing creativity. I create web content for the love of coding, long live my code! I understand and code: PHP, Java, JavaScript and of course HTML5 and CSS3. I do my magic in frameworks such as: Bootstrap,Laravel and lately the Google Web Starter Kit. I am also learning Reactjs, it's awesome. I will soon be a fullstack JavaScript developer.
Git and Github, how software is made.
Since I learned about version control, git has been a very important tool in my toolbelt. I have been actively using git for version controlling my projects since October 2016. Man is not an Island of himself, I believe in the strength within collaboration. Thanks to Github for making this possible. Connect with me on Github.
Linux, the super user!
Since I started using Ubuntu in February 2016, never before had I experienced the power of computers. I love the open source community and I will never be thankful enough to Linus Tovalds for initiating such a beautiful project. After being on so many youtube tutorials, I have learned how to effectively use the linux terminal and I am now acquinted to majority of the terminal commands. I also write shell scripts and when I am sad, I hack!
